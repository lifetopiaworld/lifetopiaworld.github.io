How to Play

Before You Play

Prerequisites

  • Wallet network set to Solana Devnet
  • Have Devnet SOL tokens
  • Get free tokens from faucet.solana.com
    • Open Solana Faucet

Getting Started

  • Click Connect on main screen
  • Connect your Solana wallet
  • Select the Farm Map

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
  • M — World Map
  • Backspace — Backpack
  • 1–9 / Scroll — Change slot

Extras

  • Autosave enabled
  • Claim Starter Pack NFT for seeds
Step 1

Connect Wallet & Enter Farm

Connect your Solana wallet, then select the Farm Map to enter the world and begin your farming journey.

Step 2

Till, Water & Plant Crops

Use the shovel to till soil, water the land, plant seeds, and harvest mature crops using the scythe.

Step 3

Use Tools on Resources

Grass requires a scythe, rocks a pickaxe, and trees an axe to gather materials.

Step 4

Trade & Buy Seeds

Talk to NPCs to open shops, buy new seeds and tools with gold, or sell items from inventory.

Step 5

Explore & Grow Your Farm

Expand your land, gather resources, improve equipment, and watch your farm evolve.

Gameplay Experience

Lifetopia World blends relaxing activities with meaningful progression, allowing you to play at your own pace while constantly discovering new possibilities.

Relaxing Daily Activities

Farm, fish, cook, craft, or simply explore. Every activity contributes to your growth without forcing a rigid playstyle.

Meaningful Progression

Unlock new tools, skills, and opportunities as you invest time into the world, creating a sense of steady and satisfying growth.

Play Solo or Together

Enjoy peaceful solo sessions or team up with friends for shared adventures, cooperative goals, and community events.

Designed for Long-Term Enjoyment

No pressure, no rush, just a living world that evolves alongside you, rewarding consistency rather than grind.

Playable MVP

Lifetopia World has successfully delivered a playable Pre-Alpha prototype focused on core farming gameplay.

The MVP demonstrates essential systems including resource gathering, crop growth, inventory management, tool usage, NPC interaction, and a persistent farm environment.

This playable vertical slice validates gameplay experience, technical feasibility, and the long-term vision of a player-driven social sandbox world.

Development is now progressing toward a Public Alpha release, expanding into multiplayer features, social systems, and a full player-driven economy.

Player-Driven Economy

Lifetopia World features a living economy shaped entirely by players. Every crop harvested, item crafted, and service provided contributes to a dynamic ecosystem where effort, creativity, and cooperation determine success.

Earn Through Gameplay

Progress is driven by meaningful activities: farming, crafting, trading, and exploration all generate value within the world.

Supply & Demand

Resources are not infinite. Player production directly influences availability, encouraging specialization, cooperation, and smart decision-making.

Trade Freely

Exchange goods, materials, and services with other players in an open marketplace designed to reward effort and skill, not pay-to-win mechanics.

Long-Term Value

Your time investment matters. Assets and progress contribute to a persistent world designed for longevity rather than short-term grinding.

Built to support a sustainable and fair ecosystem, Lifetopia World prioritizes player experience first, creating a world where growth feels natural, rewarding, and meaningful over time.

Development Roadmap

Phase 1 — Completed

Playable MVP (Pre-Alpha)

Delivery of a fully playable farming vertical slice, including core gameplay systems, resource gathering, inventory management, tool usage, NPC interaction, and a persistent farm environment.

Phase 2 — In Development

Public Alpha Release

Expansion of the MVP into a broader playable experience with multiplayer foundations, improved persistence, gameplay polish, content additions, and overall stability improvements.

Phase 3

Social Sandbox Features

Introduction of player interaction systems, world visits, cooperative gameplay, community activities, and shared progression features to transform the game into a social sandbox experience.

Phase 4

Player-Driven Economy

Implementation of trading systems, advanced crafting, marketplace features, and long-term progression loops supported by a sustainable player-driven economy.

Future Vision

Persistent Living World

A fully evolving social sandbox where players shape the world through their actions, communities, and economic activities over time.
